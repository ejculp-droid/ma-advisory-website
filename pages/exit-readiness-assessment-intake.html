The Exit Readiness Assessment

Know Where Your Business Stands Before the Market Tells You

Are you prepared to exit on your own terms, or would a buyer uncover gaps that reduce value, delay closing, or weaken your leverage? The RTO Exit Readiness Assessment gives business owners a clear, buyer-informed view of where their company stands across the six readiness gaps that most often affect transferability, deal certainty, and enterprise value.

Through The RTO Exit Readiness Index™, we convert exit preparation into one defensible readiness score, a buyer-style heat map, and a practical action plan across each gap. Request your assessment to see where value is protected, where risk is exposed, and what steps can improve your position before a transaction, succession event, or unsolicited offer forces the issue.
