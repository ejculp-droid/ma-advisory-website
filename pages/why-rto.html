Why Business Owners Choose RTO Advisory

Your life's work deserves more than a quick sale. Discover why smart business owners choose fee-based strategic advisory over commission-driven brokers.

The Challenge You're Facing

You've spent decades building a business that represents far more than financial statements—it's your reputation, your employees' security, and the legacy you'll leave behind. Yet as you contemplate your exit, you face a sobering reality:

The odds are stacked against you:

  • 60% of business owners have no succession plan
  • Only 30% of businesses listed for sale ever find a buyer
  • Traditional brokers charge 8-12% commissions ($300,000+ on a $5M sale) while prioritizing quick deals over optimal outcomes
  • Less than 15% of family businesses successfully transition to the next generation

You're left wondering:

  • How do I maximize the value of my life's work?
  • How do I know if I'm truly ready for a transaction?
  • How do I evaluate offers from PE firms or strategic buyers who've approached me?
  • How do I coordinate attorneys, accountants, bankers, and other advisors through this complex process?
  • How do I protect my employees and maintain our culture?

How RTO Advisory Solves This

We address each challenge with a fundamentally different approach:

Challenge 1: You Don't Know Where to Start

32% of owners cite "lack of knowledge on where to start" as their biggest barrier

Our Solution:

We begin with a comprehensive business assessment and develop a customized 2-5 year roadmap that transforms your business from where it is today to maximum market readiness. You'll know exactly what needs to happen, when, and why—regardless of whether you already have a buyer in mind or are just beginning to explore your options.

Challenge 2: You've Been Approached, But Don't Know If It's the Right Deal

PE firms, strategic acquirers, or brokers have reached out, but you're unsure if their offer is fair or if you're prepared

Our Solution:

We provide independent transaction readiness assessment and offer evaluation to help you understand:

  • Whether your business is truly buyer-ready or if preparation would increase value
  • How the offer compares to market benchmarks and your business's potential
  • What gaps exist in financial reporting, operations, or documentation
  • Whether now is the right time or if strategic preparation would yield better outcomes

You gain clarity and leverage—whether you proceed, decline, or negotiate from a position of strength.

Challenge 3: You're Too Busy Running the Business

45% of owners say they're "too busy" to plan their exit

Our Solution:

We work alongside you and your team, implementing improvements without disrupting daily operations. Our systematic approach handles the strategic planning while you maintain focus on running your business profitably.

Challenge 4: You're Worried About Leaving Money on the Table

Most owners underestimate their business value or accept suboptimal deals due to lack of preparation

Our Solution:

Our multi-year value optimization process systematically enhances your business worth through financial performance improvements, operational efficiency, and strategic positioning. Businesses in the lower middle market sell for 4-15x EBITDA—proper preparation can mean millions in additional proceeds.

Getting buyer-ready means more than clean financials—it means documented processes, scalable operations, strong management teams, and strategic positioning that maximizes competitive tension and valuation multiples.

Challenge 5: You're Overwhelmed By the Complexity

Exit transactions require coordinating attorneys, CPAs, wealth advisors, lenders, insurance professionals, and industry specialists

Our Solution:

We serve as your transaction quarterback—the single point of coordination managing all professional service providers throughout the entire process. We:

  • Assemble and manage your advisory team
  • Coordinate due diligence requests and responses
  • Ensure all parties stay aligned on timeline and objectives
  • Translate complex legal, tax, and financial concepts into actionable decisions
  • Keep the process moving forward while you focus on business operations

You maintain one relationship with one trusted advisor, while we orchestrate the symphony of specialists required for a successful exit.

Challenge 6: You Fear Losing Control of Your Legacy

Your business reflects your values, standards, and commitment to employees and community

Our Solution:

Whether you're working with a buyer you've identified, evaluating inbound offers, or preparing for a future transaction, we help you:

  • Define your non-negotiable legacy priorities upfront
  • Structure transactions that protect what matters most
  • Evaluate buyer cultural fit and operational philosophy
  • Negotiate employment protections for key team members
  • Design earn-outs and transition agreements that preserve institutional knowledge

Challenge 7: You Don't Trust Commission-Based Brokers

Traditional brokers earn more when deals close quickly, creating conflicts with your long-term interests

Our Solution:

Our fee-based advisory model ($25,000-$75,000 retainers) means we earn compensation for providing sound strategic advice—not for rushing you into suboptimal transactions. We save you 50-90% versus traditional broker commissions while ensuring recommendations serve only your interests.

Important distinction: We're not a matchmaking service or buyer-sourcing firm. We're strategic advisors who prepare you for success regardless of how your buyer emerges—whether through your own relationships, inbound approaches, or if you later choose to engage a broker or investment banker for formal buyer identification.

Challenge 8: You Want to Exit on Your Terms

You've earned the right to retire when and how you choose, not when the market or a buyer dictates

Our Solution:

Early planning gives you control. Whether you're:

  • Exploring options with no immediate plans
  • Evaluating an unsolicited offer from a PE firm or strategic buyer
  • Working with a specific buyer already in discussions
  • Planning a transaction 2-5 years out

...we meet you where you are and create a roadmap that puts you in the strongest possible position.
