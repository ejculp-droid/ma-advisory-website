Why Fee-Based vs. Commission-Based?

Commission-Based Brokers

Commission-Based Brokers | 8–12% of Sale Price

  • Conflicts of Interest:

    Incentivized to push for a quick sale

  • Timing Pressure:

    Motivation to sell now versus when timing is optimal

  • Lower Valuations:

    Can still be acceptable because any sale creates commission

  • Limited Incentive:

    To improve business value before a transaction

  • Fees Scale:

    With transaction size whether or not the added cost is justified

  • No True Objectivity:

    Advice is shaped by the incentive to close, not by what is best for the owner

Fee-Based Advisory

Fee-Based Advisory | RTO Advisory Model

  • Aligned Interests:

    Paid for advice, not for forcing a sale

  • Optimal Timing:

    Recommend selling only when the timing is truly right

  • Value Maximization:

    Focus on the best possible owner outcome

  • Strategic Preparation:

    Multi-year value enhancement support

  • Transparent Costs:

    Fee expectations known up front

  • No Pressure:

    Guidance based solely on your best interests
