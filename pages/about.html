About RTO Advisory

RTO Advisory serves owners who expect disciplined execution, strategic clarity, and stewardship-level responsibility throughout the life of the business and the transaction.

Our Approach

Everything we do centers on disciplined, intentional preparation. Strong outcomes are engineered through financial and operational refinement, strategic positioning, due diligence readiness, thoughtful deal structuring, and coordinated collaboration with legal, tax, financial, and operational stakeholders.

Our guiding principle is straightforward: steward the business as if it were our own. Every recommendation, negotiation, and structural decision is approached with the same diligence, rigor, and accountability we would apply to our own enterprise.

Who We Work With

RTO Advisory is not designed for every owner, and that selectivity is intentional. We hold ourselves to a high standard and expect the same level of discipline and engagement from the clients we represent. Selling, buying, or merging a lifetime of work is not part-time work.

Full engagement enables us to operate decisively, anticipate issues before they surface, and reduce the number of stakeholders owners must personally manage. The deeper the engagement, the stronger the alignment and the more effectively we can quarterback the process from start to finish.

Why We Exist

RTO Advisory exists to serve owners who require more than transactional representation. Our purpose is to maximize enterprise value while protecting relationships, culture, and the long-term integrity of the business.

Our retainer-based advisory structure ensures independence and alignment. Rather than prioritizing speed or volume, we focus on multi-year value creation, strengthening financial performance, refining strategy, enhancing operational discipline, and preparing leadership and systems for transition.

When the timing is appropriate, we lead the transaction process with structure, clarity, and control.

What We Do

When a transaction becomes the right course of action, we act as the owner's deal captain, driving execution, coordinating advisors, managing diligence, structuring negotiations, and protecting the owner's position throughout the process.

Our work includes pre-transaction readiness, multi-year value enhancement, sell-side preparation, buy-side advisory, financial modeling, transaction structuring, succession planning, and full M&A execution through closing. We operate across industries including manufacturing, logistics, transportation, financial services, technology, and professional services.

Our Commitment

Our commitment is unwavering: treat the business with the stewardship, diligence, and respect it deserves. Owners rely on us for disciplined execution, objective guidance, and an integrated understanding of both financial and organizational dynamics.

"Return to Owner" reflects our operating philosophy. We center the owner's objectives, protect what they have built, and deliver outcomes aligned with their long-term priorities. For owners who expect rigor, accountability, and a partner who operates with ownership-level responsibility, RTO Advisory provides that standard.

About the Founder

Elliott Culp is the Founder of RTO Advisory. He brings more than a decade of experience in commercial banking, wealth management, and transaction advisory. His work has included capital structure evaluation, credit analysis, financial modeling, transaction structuring, due diligence coordination, and strategic advisory for privately held companies across multiple industries.

He has guided owners through mergers, acquisitions, divestitures, and succession planning initiatives, providing financial oversight and strategic direction throughout the transaction lifecycle. His experience spans manufacturing, logistics, transportation, financial services, technology, and professional service firms, offering a cross-sector perspective on enterprise value creation and transition strategy.

Elliott holds degrees in psychology and sociology and earned an MBA in international business and finance, combining analytical discipline with a clear understanding of the human dynamics inherent in business transition.
