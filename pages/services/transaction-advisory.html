Transaction Types

Every Path to a Transaction

From strategic corporate acquisitions to family succession and private equity, we guide owners through every transaction structure.

Strategic Sales

Corporate acquisitions, management buyouts (MBOs), and recapitalizations where a third-party strategic buyer acquires a controlling or complete interest in the business.

Financial Sponsor

Private equity acquisitions, search fund purchases, and family office investments where a financial buyer takes a controlling position with a defined investment horizon.

Succession

Family transfers and gifting strategies, management equity transitions, and partial or staged exits that preserve ownership continuity through a structured internal transfer.
