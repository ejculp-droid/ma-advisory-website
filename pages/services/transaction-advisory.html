Transaction Advisory & Management

Whether you've received an unsolicited offer, identified a potential buyer, or are preparing to engage the market, navigating an M&A transaction requires specialized expertise and disciplined process management.

RTO Advisory serves as your transaction quarterback—coordinating all advisors, managing the process, protecting your interests, and ensuring you achieve optimal terms while preserving your legacy.

Our Transaction Advisory Services

Offer Evaluation & Negotiation Strategy

When you've received an inbound offer or LOI:

  • Independent valuation and fairness assessment
  • Deal structure analysis (cash, earnouts, seller notes, equity rollover)
  • Comparable transaction benchmarking
  • Negotiation strategy and leverage points
  • Red flag identification

Business Valuation & Positioning

  • Comprehensive enterprise valuation (DCF, market multiples, asset-based)
  • Investment memorandum/CIM development
  • Management presentation creation
  • Financial model and projections
  • Industry and competitive analysis

Due Diligence Management

  • Due diligence request list preparation
  • Virtual data room organization
  • Response coordination across all advisors
  • Issue identification and resolution
  • Confidentiality management

Deal Structure Optimization

  • Tax-efficient transaction structuring
  • Earnout and performance milestone design
  • Employment and non-compete agreements
  • Seller financing and rollover equity terms
  • Risk allocation and indemnification

Professional Team Coordination

We serve as your single point of contact, managing:

  • M&A attorneys (purchase agreement, disclosure schedules)
  • Tax advisors (structure optimization, tax planning)
  • Wealth managers (proceeds deployment planning)
  • Industry consultants and specialists
  • Quality of earnings providers
  • Environmental and regulatory consultants

Closing & Transition Support

  • Closing checklist and timeline management
  • Document review and execution coordination
  • Funds transfer and escrow management
  • Post-closing transition planning
  • Integration support and knowledge transfer

Transaction Types We Support

When to Engage Us

Scenario 1: You've Received an Offer

We provide independent evaluation within 2 weeks to help you understand if it's fair, what leverage you have, and whether to proceed.

Scenario 2: You Have a Buyer in Mind

We structure the engagement, coordinate due diligence, and negotiate terms that protect your interests and legacy priorities.

Scenario 3: You're Preparing to Transact

We develop your positioning materials, coordinate advisors, and manage the entire process from preparation through closing.

Our Transaction Philosophy

  • Independence First - Fee-based advice aligned with your interests
  • Legacy Protection - Transaction structures that honor your priorities
  • Process Discipline - Rigorous project management and timeline control
  • Advisor Coordination - Single quarterback managing all specialists
  • Confidentiality - Strict protocols protecting business operations
  • Strategic Flexibility - Walk-away power through thorough preparation
